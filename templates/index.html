Mango Leaf Diseases

Anthracnose

Causes: Fungal infection caused by Colletotrichum species.

Symptoms: Dark, sunken lesions on leaves, stems, and fruits.

Prevention: Use resistant varieties, maintain good airflow, and apply fungicides as needed.

Cutting Weevil

Causes: Infestation by the weevil species.

Symptoms: Leaves cut off at the edges, stunted growth.

Prevention: Remove debris, use traps, and apply insecticides as needed.

Die Back

Causes: Fungal pathogens or environmental stress.

Symptoms: Browning of leaf tips, dieback of branches.

Prevention: Prune infected branches, improve drainage, and manage watering.

Powdery Mildew

Causes: Fungal pathogens in humid conditions.

Symptoms: White powdery spots on leaves.

Prevention: Ensure adequate sunlight, improve air circulation, and use fungicides if necessary.
